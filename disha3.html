About Us

Team Disha

Team Disha is a dedicated group of individuals committed to making a positive impact on the lives of the less fortunate, especially those who are visually impaired. Their mission revolves around extending a helping hand to the poor, particularly focusing on the needs of blind individuals. The team is driven by a profound sense of compassion and a desire to bring about positive change in the community through selfless service, or "sewa."
